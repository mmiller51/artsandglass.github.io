Welcome to Arts & Glass

We are a fully walk-in art studio where you can create your own masterpiece of paint-your-own pottery or glass fusion, no matter your artistic skill.
Once you've finished creating your piece we take care of the rest and your piece is ready for pickup in 7-10 days depending on our kiln schedule.
Besides for a walk-in basis, we also hold classes for kids and adults, summer camps as well as spaces for birthday parties. We also have gift cards for purchase in-store and online. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to stop in or call us. You can reach us at our contact page: the Kiln Room.
