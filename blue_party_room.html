Summer Camps

Our summer camps are drop-off programs that take place over 3 day sessions and run from 10-12:30 each day of the session. Each session costs $115 per camper and includes 3 projects (glass and pottery) as well as extra non-fired crafts and activities and a pizza party on the last day. Campers are welcome to bring snacks but we ask that peanuts are avoided for allergy reasons.

Kids Classes

Often called Kids Night Out, our kids classes are a drop-off class that includes pizza, a juice box and a fully instructed lesson on a specific project. Our staff is friendly and will always try our best to help any kid make a masterpiece. The price of the class varies depending on the project being made but it covers the project, all of the supplies, the 2 hours spent in the studio, and of course the pizza and juice box! Keep an eye out and follow us on our Facebook Page for updates on new and upcoming classes!

Adult Classes

Wine and Design Nights are open to any adult who want to relax, have a drink, and make some art with us. Pay per glass/drink but enjoy complementary snacks and desserts. The price of the class varies depending on the project being taught. We do both pottery and glass fusion projects as well as wood board art projects which are not offered as a walk-in project.
