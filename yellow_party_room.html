Celebrate With Us!

Here at Arts & Glass, we love celebrating and helping you celebrate! Whether you're celebrating a birthday, getting some co-workers together, or just want to hang out with a group of friends, booking one of our party rooms is a great option for you!

Kids Parties

All kid's parties include:

  • Room for 10-24 artists and up to 4 non-painters including family members
  • 90 Minutes in one of our private party rooms (For an additional fee, you can purchase an extra half hour if you feel necessary)
  • At least one personal party host (cleanup included!)
  • All paints, tools and the firing process
  • An instructional art lesson
  • A FREE personalized keepsake ceramic plate for the birthday child
Any pieces made during the party are available for pickup by either the individual guest or the birthday family in approximately 10 days.
We also have pre-made Arts & Glass Goody Bags available for puchase. For only $4.95 per guest, we will prepare candy-free goody bags filled with creative and artistic tools like notebooks, fun-shaped erasers, stickers and more!

Adult Parties & Room Rental

Whether its a group meeting, a birthday, or a team-building event, have your event here with us! You are more than welcome to bring your own food or snacks and non-alocholic beverages to help celebrate any occasion. We do have alcohol on site available for purchase. Please contact the studio for questions about alochol availability. We can also help you develop a theme for your projects or everyone can create freely their own piece of choice. One of our friendly staff will be on hand to help your group through the creative process.
